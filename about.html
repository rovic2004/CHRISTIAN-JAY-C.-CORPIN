About Tomalistis Falls

Tomalistis Falls, often referred to as "The Fountain of Youth," is a breathtaking waterfall located in the serene landscapes of Leyte. Renowned for its crystal-clear waters and untouched surroundings, it is a hidden gem perfect for nature enthusiasts and adventurers alike.

Surrounded by lush greenery and cascading waters, Tomalistis Falls offers visitors a peaceful retreat to reconnect with nature. Its unique beauty and tranquil ambiance make it a must-visit destination in the Philippines.

Why Visit Tomalistis Falls?

Natural Beauty

Marvel at the mesmerizing waterfalls, pristine waters, and lush vegetation.

Adventure

Engage in hiking, swimming, and exploring the surrounding forest trails.

Relaxation

Experience a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
